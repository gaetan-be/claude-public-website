The Invitation

It all started with a simple text message. "Hey, want to grab dinner tonight?" Emma typed, not knowing this would become their most memorable evening yet. Her best friend Lily replied instantly with an enthusiastic "YES!"

They met at their favorite spot, a cozy bistro tucked away in the old part of town. The warm glow of candles flickered on the tables, and the aroma of freshly baked bread filled the air.

Hours flew by as they shared stories, dreams, and countless laughs. They talked about everything and nothing, the way only true friends can.
