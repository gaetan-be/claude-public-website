AGE OF EMPIRES II

Web Edition

How to Play

Controls

  • Left Click — Select unit / building
  • Right Click — Move / Attack / Gather
  • Click + Drag — Box-select units
  • WASD / Arrows — Scroll map
  • Scroll Wheel — Zoom in / out
  • Escape — Deselect / Cancel build

Resources

  • 🌾 Food — Berries, Farms
  • 🌲 Wood — Trees
  • 💰 Gold — Gold Mines
  • 🪨 Stone — Stone Mines
  • 👥 Population — Build Houses

Units

  • Villager — Builds & gathers
  • Militia — Basic melee fighter
  • Archer — Ranged attacker
  • Knight — Heavy cavalry

Buildings

  • Town Center — Train villagers
  • House — +5 population cap
  • Barracks — Train military
  • Lumber Camp — Wood drop-off
  • Mining Camp — Gold/Stone drop-off
  • Farm — Food source

🏆 Win: Destroy all enemy buildings!
